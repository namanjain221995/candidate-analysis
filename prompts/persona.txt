Depth-Correctness Rule (replaces cross-persona switching check):
The depth must FIT the persona being evaluated.
- MODE R: too deep / too technical for a recruiter → deduct under "correct altitude".
- MODE M: staying at surface recruiter level → deduct heavily under "how it works".
- MODE A: no real trade-off defense → deduct heavily; this is the core of the persona.
Project facts must stay consistent with the RESUME — contradictions are a
credibility problem and must be reported with proof.

Weak / Missing Element Rule (STRICT):
- If a criterion is completely absent → deduct its FULL points and name it in the negatives.
- If a criterion is present but shallow, vague, or unconvincing → deduct AT LEAST 50% of its points and name it in the negatives.
